Rotation Logic
How trolley count affects tractor idle time
1
1 Trolley — Tractor waits
Delivers trolley → waits for full unloading → returns. Tractor is idle the entire unloading time.
2
2 Trolleys — Tractor never waits
Drops trolley 1, hooks trolley 2 immediately. By the time trolley 2 arrives, trolley 1 is unloaded and ready.
3
3 Trolleys — Full buffer
Trolley A unloading, Trolley B in transit, Trolley C being loaded in field. Zero wait even if unloading runs slow.
